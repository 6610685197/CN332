# Community Sync

Community Sync is a web-based system for managing residential communities and shared living environments.  
It helps administrators and residents handle daily tasks like payments, maintenance requests, visitor management, and announcements in one central platform.

## Project Goal
The goal of this project is to make community management simpler and more organized.  
Instead of relying on multiple tools such as paper notices, chat applications, or spreadsheets, all essential tasks can be managed in one system.

---

## Users
- Community / Property administrators  
- Juristic person committee members  
- Residents  

---

## Features

### Dashboard
- Provides an overview of the community in one place  
- Displays financial status, maintenance requests, complaints, and announcements  

---

### Fee Management
- Notifies residents about outstanding payments  
- Supports online payments  
- Automatically generates receipts  

---

### Maintenance System
- Residents can submit repair requests through the system  
- Track maintenance status in real time  
- View maintenance history  

---

### Visitor Management
- Replaces physical card usage with an application-based system  
- Security staff receives visitors and records their ID information  
- Residents are notified through the app when a visitor arrives  
- Residents confirm or deny access directly in the app instead of stamping a physical card  

---

### Announcements & Communication
- Send announcements to residents  
- Notifications via application, email, or LINE  
- Used for important updates and community news  

---

### Reports & Documents
- Financial and operational reports  
- Digital document storage  

---
